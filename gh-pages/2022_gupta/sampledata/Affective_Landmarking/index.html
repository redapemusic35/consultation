Doccano Annotation Tutorial for Meha Gupta Sentiment Annotation Project

  1. The "site" is up and "running" at https://new-gupta.herokuapp.com/. You will likely have been added as an "annotator".

  2. You will have been given standard permissions meaning that you should be able to make annotations and what not by logging in using username and pw.

  3. Please log in. Click on the project titled "Gupta Trial" and then select "start annotation" in top left corner of the page.

  4. To annotate, highlight a section of text. A box will populate underneath that with 5 emotions numbered 0-4. Arbitrarily select one of those and continue as far as you have time to. You can move to the next document by selected the '>' near the top right corner of the page.
